## 📚 Statistical Analysis

### Business Question

**Does Deal Size significantly affect Sales?**

### Null Hypothesis (H₀)

There is no significant difference in average sales between Large and Small deals.

### Alternative Hypothesis (H₁)

There is a significant difference in average sales between Large and Small deals.

### Statistical Test

Independent Samples t-test

### Results

- **T-Statistic:** 106.4993
- **P-Value:** 0.0000

### Conclusion

Since the p-value is less than 0.05, the null hypothesis was rejected.

The analysis confirms that Large Deals generate significantly higher sales than Small Deals. This finding suggests that encouraging customers toward larger purchases can positively impact overall sales performance.
